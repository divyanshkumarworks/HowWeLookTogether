How It Works

  1. Upload your images and enter your respective heights.
  2. The app will crop out the persons from their original images.
  3. The images will be resized based on your provided heights.
  4. The app will merge the resized images side by side.
  5. The merged image will be placed on a default background image.
  6. Preview the final result and save it for sharing.
